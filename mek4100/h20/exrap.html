Comments
  • Points 1 2a and 2c(i) were generally well answered.
  • The overall score on Point 2c(ii) corresponds to E. Problem 2e is identical to problem 22 in the leaflet and is a very basic two scale problem. In view of this the score on 2c(ii) is disappointing.
  • Problem 3 is a rather simple problem on Hamiltonian mechanics. The candidate were not well prepared on this topic.
  • Problem 4 has the lowest score. This is not surprising or alarming. However, after differentiation the problem becomes a special case of problem 8 from the leaflet.
