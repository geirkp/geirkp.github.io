The exam MEK4320 spring 2020

  • Date: June 10th.
  • It will be a home exam utilizing the cloud meeting tool zoom .
The exam is oral and each candidate is questioned for 30 minutes. This time will be strictly observed. After each candidate the examiners will confer shortly. The assessment is pass/non-pass and the requirement for pass corresponds to the mark E.
All candidates must be ready to join the zoom meeting at her indicated time. They must also have paper and pen available.
